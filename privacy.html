Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Who we are

MAC Cleaning Limited ("we", "us", "our") is a window cleaning service operating across Luton, Bedfordshire and Hertfordshire. Our contact number is 01582 858 588.

What data we collect

When you use our quote tool or sign up for our service, we collect:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, and property address
  • Property details (type, size, extras) to calculate your price
  • Payment card details (processed securely by Stripe — we never see or store your full card number)

How we use your data

We use your personal data to:

  • Provide you with a quote and deliver our cleaning services
  • Send you appointment reminders by text message
  • Process payments after each clean via Stripe
  • Send you invoices and receipts by email
  • Contact you about your service (e.g. schedule changes, weather delays)

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not send marketing emails unless you opt in.

Payment processing

Card payments are handled by Stripe, a PCI-DSS Level 1 certified payment processor. Your card details are encrypted and stored by Stripe, not by us. We only see the last four digits of your card for identification purposes.

Data storage and security

Your data is stored securely and we take reasonable measures to protect it. We retain your data for as long as you are a customer and for up to 6 years afterwards for accounting and legal purposes.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Withdraw consent for text message reminders at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us on 01582 858 588 or via WhatsApp.

Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies only for basic site functionality. We may use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. No advertising or tracking cookies are used.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes.
