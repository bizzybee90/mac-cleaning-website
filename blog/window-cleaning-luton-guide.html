Window Cleaning in Luton: Complete Guide, Prices & How to Sign Up (2026)

20 March 2026 · 8 min read

If you live in Luton, you already know how quickly windows lose their shine. Between the M1 corridor kicking up road dust, flights in and out of London Luton coating everything in fine particulate, and the ongoing town centre regeneration stirring up building dust, your glass is fighting a losing battle. This guide covers everything you need to know about getting your windows professionally cleaned in Luton — what it costs, what's included, and how to get started with us at MAC Cleaning.

Why Luton Homes Need Regular Window Cleaning

Luton sits in a uniquely challenging spot for keeping windows clean. The M1 runs right along the western edge of town, and prevailing winds carry road grime across Bramingham, Marsh Farm, and Limbury. If you're under the flight path — and large parts of Stopsley, Farley Hill, and Round Green are — you'll notice a thin, oily film on exterior glass that builds up surprisingly fast.

Then there's the water. Bedfordshire sits on a chalk aquifer, which means the tap water is hard. Really hard. If you've ever tried cleaning your own windows with a hosepipe or a bucket of tap water, you'll have seen the white mineral deposits it leaves behind once it dries. That's calcium carbonate, and it bonds to glass over time, becoming increasingly difficult to shift the longer you leave it.

The town centre regeneration — the Power Court development, Arndale House demolition area, and ongoing works around the station — means construction dust is a fact of life for anyone in central Luton, Biscot, or Bury Park. Even if you're further out in Stopsley or Sundon Park, the dust finds its way around.

All of this is why we recommend a regular 4-weekly clean for most Luton homes. It keeps on top of the build-up before it bonds to the glass, and it actually works out cheaper per visit than less frequent cleans.

What's Included in Every Clean

When we say "window cleaning," we mean the full job — not just a quick wipe of the panes. Every visit includes:

  • All exterior windows — front, back, side, upstairs, downstairs
  • Frames, sills, and doors cleaned as standard (front door and back door glass)
  • Water-fed pole system — we work from the ground using telescopic carbon-fibre poles, so there are no ladders propped against your house for the vast majority of homes
  • Purified water only — our van-mounted system filters water through a four-stage purification process that strips out all the minerals. No chemicals, no washing-up liquid, no streaks. The water dries spot-free because there's nothing left in it to leave marks
  • Text reminder the evening before — so you know we're coming and can move any bins or garden furniture away from the windows

After each clean, we take a photo as confirmation, and your payment is processed automatically. You don't need to leave out cash or remember to transfer anything.

Common Questions About Window Cleaning in Luton

Do I need to be home?

No. The vast majority of our cleans are done entirely from outside using water-fed poles. We don't need access to your house — just make sure the side gate is unlocked if you have windows round the back. We'll text you when the job is done.

What if it rains?

We clean in the rain. Because we use purified water with zero mineral content, rainwater won't leave marks on freshly cleaned glass. Rain is just water — it's the dissolved minerals in tap water and the dirt on the glass that cause streaks. Once the glass is clean, rain simply runs off. We only postpone in genuinely unsafe conditions like high winds or electrical storms.

How do I pay?

By card, automatically. When you sign up, you add a debit or credit card. After each clean, we charge the agreed amount. You'll get a notification each time. No cash, no bank transfers, no chasing. It keeps things simple for everyone.

Can I add gutter clearing?

Absolutely. We offer gutter clearing, fascia and soffit cleaning, and conservatory roof cleaning as add-on services. Most customers add a gutter clear once or twice a year — typically in late autumn after the leaves have fallen and again in spring. You can request it any time through the reminder text or by calling us on 01582 858 588.

What about interior windows?

Our standard service covers exteriors only, which is where the real grime builds up. If you'd like interiors done as well — say before a special occasion or as part of a spring clean — just let us know and we can arrange it as a one-off at an additional charge.

I've never had a professional window cleaner before. Is the first clean more expensive?

Sometimes, yes. If your windows haven't been professionally cleaned in a long time, the first visit can take significantly longer due to built-up hard water staining and grime. We'll let you know if a surcharge applies when we see the property — it's typically a one-off uplift on the first clean only. After that, you're on the standard price.
