How We Clean Your Conservatory Roof

Safe, effective exterior cleaning from the ground — we never climb on your conservatory.

Water-Fed Poles

Our telescopic poles reach over your conservatory roof from ground level. No climbing, no risk of damage to roof panels.

Purified Water

De-ionised water dissolves algae and grime without chemicals. Safe for both polycarbonate and glass panels — dries spot-free.

Exterior Only

We clean the outside of your conservatory roof where algae and moss build up. This is where 99% of the dirt and discolouration sits.

Safe for All Roofs

Polycarbonate, glass, self-cleaning glass — our gentle process is safe for all conservatory roof types. No pressure washing.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it safe for polycarbonate roofs?
Yes. Our water-fed pole system is gentle enough for polycarbonate and glass. No pressure washing.
Do you clean the inside too?
No, this service covers the outside of the conservatory roof only. The outside is where algae, moss, and bird droppings build up.
How often should it be done?
Most conservatories benefit from an annual clean. North-facing or tree-shaded roofs may need it more often.
Will you clean the conservatory windows too?
The roof clean covers the roof panels only. We can add conservatory windows to your regular window cleaning round for £9 extra per visit.
How long does it take?
Most conservatory roofs take 1-2 hours.
