Terms of Business

Last updated: March 2026

1. The service

MAC Cleaning Limited provides exterior window cleaning and related property maintenance services (gutter clearing, fascia and soffit cleaning, conservatory roof cleaning) across Luton, Bedfordshire and Hertfordshire.

2. Pricing and quotes

Prices are calculated based on property type, size, and selected services. The price shown in your online quote is fixed for the duration of your service unless we notify you of a change with at least 30 days' notice. One-off service prices are agreed at the time of booking.

3. Scheduling

Regular window cleaning customers are assigned to a cleaning day. You will receive a text message the night before each scheduled visit. We aim to clean on the same day each cycle, but may occasionally adjust routes due to weather or logistics. We will always notify you of significant changes.

4. Payment

  • All payments are by card only. We do not accept cash or bank transfers.
  • Your card is registered securely via Stripe when you sign up.
  • Payment is taken automatically after each clean is completed.
  • You will receive an email receipt for every payment.
  • For one-off services, payment is taken upon completion of the work.

5. Cancellations and skipped cleans

  • You may skip a clean free of charge by notifying us at least 24 hours before your scheduled appointment.
  • Cancellations made on the day of your appointment may incur the full charge, as your time slot has already been allocated to our team.
  • To cancel your service entirely, contact us by phone, WhatsApp, or email. There is no contract, no notice period, and no cancellation fee.

6. Weather

We clean in light rain as our purified water system produces streak-free results in wet conditions. In heavy rain, storms, or dangerous conditions, we will reschedule your clean. You are never charged for a clean that does not take place.

7. Access

We require clear access to all exterior windows. Please ensure gates are unlocked and any obstructions (bins, vehicles, garden furniture) are moved on your cleaning day. If we cannot access your property, we may need to skip the clean and the full charge may still apply.

8. What's included

Every regular clean includes all exterior windows, frames, sills, and doors as standard. There is no additional charge for frames and sills. Optional extras (conservatories, extensions, porches, garages, skylights) are priced individually at sign-up and included on every visit.

9. Satisfaction guarantee

If you're not happy with the quality of a clean, contact us within 24 hours and we will return to re-clean at no additional charge.

10. Liability

MAC Cleaning Limited holds public liability insurance. We take care to avoid damage, but in the unlikely event of an issue, please notify us within 48 hours so we can investigate and resolve it.

11. Price changes

We reserve the right to adjust prices. Any changes will be communicated with at least 30 days' written notice. If you do not wish to continue at the new price, you may cancel your service at any time.
